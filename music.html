Music

An Escapism

Music is my go-to escape from everyday noise and overthinking. It has a calming effect on my mind and brings a quiet sense of peace. No matter how the day has been, music helps me pause, breathe and feel present in the moment.

Mood, Emotion and Energy

One of the most beautiful things about music is how effortlessly it changes my mood. Slow, soft melodies help me relax and unwind, while high-energy beats instantly lift my spirit. Whether I need comfort, motivation or pure joy, there is always a song that fits the feeling.

My Kind of Sound

I enjoy English music across almost every genre, there are no strict boundaries. From calm, soulful tracks to full-on party anthems, I enjoy the variety. I am especially drawn to songs from the 2010s era, which feel nostalgic yet timeless and continue to resonate deeply. Some English pop artists I enjoy listening to include Taylor Swift, Ed Sheeran, Shawn Mendes and similar artists. Their music feels honest, emotional and relatable songs that stay with you long after they end.

Exploring Beyond Listening

Music is not just something I listen to, it is something I like to explore. I have tried playing instruments like the piano, xylophone, harmonium and violin, during my school days. Learning instruments feels exciting and creative, offering a hands-on way to understand music and spend time meaningfully.

A Creative and Peaceful Pastime

For me, music is more than entertainment. It is a peaceful habit, a creative outlet and a way to express emotion without words. Whether listening, learning or experimenting with instruments, music remains a beautiful way to enjoy time, nurture talent and stay connected to what feels good.
